Ideal Product Features

Lead Generation

The companies use the applications not converted into sales as a lead generator where advisors reach out to those clients as qualified leads.

After getting a quote and filling up the personal information form, but not completing the order, a "Non-commissioned" advisor will call you and ask you to make the decision to purchase the term insurance or not. They will send an email with more information including the Client Guide, Claims fact sheet, Mortgage Protection Insurance, Critical Illness Client Guide, and Critical Illness Product guide.

Manulife on the other hand places a big "Call to Action" banner for users to call an advisor and offers a chat where customers can message Licensed Insurance Advisors.
